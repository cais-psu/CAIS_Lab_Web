Ilya, Hongliang, Adelaide, and Evan attended the Modeling Estimation and Control Conference (MECC 2024) in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.
Hongliang's work was nominated for a best student paper award and he was selected as a "Rising Star" in the ASME Dynamic Systems and Control Division.
Adelaide presented a poster and a short talk on her summer work for modeling a water treatment testbed.
Ilya provided an overview of his recent CAREER award.
